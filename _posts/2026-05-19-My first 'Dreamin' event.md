Story time y'all!

As the title can tell I'm going to write about my first 'Dreamin' event. 

This event was the first edition of [Mexico Dreamin](https://mexicodreamin.com), that I wouldn't attend if it weren't by the help of [Rafa Hernández](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rafablazer) for those who don't know this awesome Jarocho, he's one of the founders of this event, a philanthropist whose efforts have helped dozens of young mexicans thrive, author of the book ['¡Soy adulto! (¿ahora qué?)'](https://www.amazon.com.mx/%C2%A1Soy-adulto-%C2%BFahora-que%CC%81-Spanish/dp/6077092231/) (I'm going to publish a review of it in a few weeks).

So, the event was held on Mérida, Yucatán city where, at that time, my mom were living and I was fortunate for not having to pay hotel for that night. Attending this event led me to a complete change in my career thanks to the connections I've made there, meeting with lots of Trailblazers from all over the world and finally talking in-person with them. This fueled my efforts and made me tackle the -now past- challenges. Here's some pictures of me over there:

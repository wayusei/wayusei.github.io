This time I'm going to make a review from a short tale made by my friend [Rafael Hernández Mercado](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rafael-mercado-3ba7b7259) for the Fanzine 'La Aldea de las Letras' Vol. 2 (April 2026).

![Fanzine La Aldea de las Letras Volumen 2 - Abril 2026]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/fanzineVol2.png)

The tale name is 'El reino en el que nadie duerme' and it goes about the kingdom of Lúmino and his prince David who learns about the power of sleep well and to give yourself time to rest of all the constant urges of responsability and the so-called 'legacy' and traditions bound to him by his father.
I would like to know more about that kingdom, hope Rafael would continue writing more about it and maybe create a long version of it.

Way to go Rafael! You're an amazing guy and I wonder if you can make this a webtoon or similar due to your artistic skills.
